The cute and tiny wide-eyed black cat.
The cute black cat that ruined my life.

Full Story

Little did I know Kitty had some top level juju. I'm pretty sure she was a hypnotist.

I gave her my toys...

And I gave her my food...

She never thanked me. Only walked away with the prized possessions. Leaving the hooman whose heart she had torn apart.

I'm not sure where she is now. She could be out in the pouring rain.

Maybe torturing another poor soul with her deception.

Or raising an army of her DECEPTIKITTYCONS!! What horror awaits the world. I'm not sure of anything.

All I know is that she MUST be stopped!!
